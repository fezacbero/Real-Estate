About Grand Polo Club & Resort

Grand Polo Club & Resort, Dubai Investment Park-2: Highlights & Overview

Emaar Grand Polo Club & Resort – A New Launch of Luxury Villas in Dubai. Emaar introduces the Grand Polo Club & Resort, a premium villa community next to The Oasis in Dubai Investment Park 2.

Starting from AED 6.2M, villas begin at 3700 sq. ft with a flexible 80/20 payment plan. Completion is expected by 2029. This new launch offers limited villas with resort-style amenities, lush landscapes, and exclusive recreational experiences.
